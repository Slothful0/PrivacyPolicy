KeepUp Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 14, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Profile Information: Age, occupation, AI knowledge level, and responses to onboarding questions
  • Usage Data: Bookmarks, reading preferences, and app interaction data

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version
  • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent
  • Subscription Data: Purchase history and subscription status via our payment processor

Third-Party Services

  • Supabase: For user authentication, data storage, and analytics
  • Superwall: For subscription management and in-app purchases
  • YouTube/Google APIs: For video content access (no personal data collected)

2. How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

  • To create and manage your account
  • To provide personalized content and recommendations
  • To sync your bookmarks and preferences across devices

Analytics and Improvement

  • To understand user behavior and improve the app
  • To analyze onboarding responses for product development
  • To measure the effectiveness of our features

Communication

  • To send important service updates and notifications
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests

Legal Compliance

  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • To protect our rights and prevent fraud

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Service Providers

We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the app:

  • Supabase: Hosts our database and provides authentication services
  • Superwall: Processes subscription payments and manages entitlements

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only use it for the purposes we specify.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated data for research and analytical purposes. This data cannot be used to identify you.

4. Data Security

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Controls: Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular Audits: Security practices are regularly reviewed and updated

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Personal data is retained while your account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Personal identifiers are removed, but anonymized data may be retained for research
  • Legal Requirements: Data may be retained longer if required by law

6. Your Rights and Choices

Account Deletion

  • Permanently delete your account and personal data
  • Remove all bookmarks and preferences
  • Anonymize onboarding responses for research purposes

Data Access and Correction

You can access and update your personal information through the app settings.

Opt-Out Options

  • Analytics: You can limit data collection through device settings
  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings

7. Children's Privacy
