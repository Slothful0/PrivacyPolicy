# KeepUp Privacy Policy **Last Updated: November 14, 2025** ## 1. Information We Collect **Personal Information** We may collect the following personal information: • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account • Profile Information: Age, occupation, AI knowledge level, and responses to onboarding questions • Usage Data: Bookmarks, reading preferences, and app interaction data **Automatically Collected Information** • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, app version • Usage Analytics: How you interact with the app, features used, time spent • Subscription Data: Purchase history and subscription status via our payment processor **Third-Party Services** We use the following third-party services that may collect information: • Supabase: For user authentication, data storage, and analytics • Superwall: For subscription management and in-app purchases • YouTube/Google APIs: For video content access (no personal data collected) ## 2. How We Use Your Information **Service Provision** • To create and manage your account • To provide personalized content and recommendations • To sync your bookmarks and preferences across devices **Analytics and Improvement** • To understand user behavior and improve the app • To analyze onboarding responses for product development • To measure the effectiveness of our features **Communication** • To send important service updates and notifications • To respond to your inquiries and support requests **Legal Compliance** • To comply with applicable laws and regulations • To protect our rights and prevent fraud ## 3. This data cannot be used to identify you. ## 4. Data Retention • Active Accounts: Personal data is retained while your account is active • Deleted Accounts: Personal identifiers are removed, but anonymized data may be retained for research • Legal Requirements: Data may be retained longer if required by law ## 6.
